About Us

A Genuine Amazon FBA Retailer Looking to Build Lasting Partnerships

Everest Yadav LLC is a legitimate, professionally registered Amazon FBA retail business based in Sheridan, Wyoming. We purchase products directly from authorized brands, local distributors, and wholesalers — and sell them to customers on Amazon.com through the Fulfilled By Amazon (FBA) programme.

We are not a middleman or dropshipper. We physically purchase inventory, send it to Amazon's fulfilment centres, and manage the complete retail process. We are looking for reliable, long-term supplier and brand relationships built on trust and mutual growth.

Our Process

How We Source & Sell

A simple, transparent process from sourcing to selling on Amazon.

1
Contact Supplier
We reach out to authorized brands, local distributors, and wholesalers to apply for a retail buying account.
2
Purchase Inventory
We place purchase orders and buy products at wholesale or trade prices using our business account and resale certificate.
3
Ship to Amazon FBA
Inventory is prepared and shipped directly to Amazon's fulfilment centres across the USA for storage and dispatch.
4
Sell on Amazon
Products are listed on Amazon.com. Amazon handles storage, packing, shipping, and customer service on our behalf.
